About

LACrimBook aims to replace West's big and expensive handbook of criminal laws with a free, digital alternative. This HTML5 app runs anywhere and works whether or not you have an internet connection.

The project is open source and is offered free of charge by the Loyola Technology and Legal Innovation Clinic under the MIT license. The source code is available on Github.
